ETC/UPS Saudi Arabia
Integrated Express Shipping, Distribution, and Supply Chain Management

Eirad Trading & Contracting Co. Ltd (ETC) is a wholly-owned Saudi company. Exclusive authorized service contractor UPS (United Parcel Service Inc. of USA) in Saudi Arabia since 1997, ETC/UPS has been offering UPS Express, Domestic Express, and Road Freight Service. ETC/UPS today offers warehousing, logistics, distribution, and consulting solutions to its customers, making it the premiere integrated express shipping, distribution, and supply chain service provider in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

ETC/UPS History synopsis:
* The license was granted in ‘1996, which was after the exhaustive process!
* Rolled out of Saudi Arabia operation in January ’1997.
* WW Exports and Domestic started in July ‘1997.
* Imports Services started in April ‘1998.
* In House Customs Clearance by July ‘1999.

ETC/UPS has grown exponentially in the last five (5) years, and now covers almost 95% of the domestic market, in addition to the 220 territories worldwide. ETC/UPS has created many products and services to meet the growing demands of the local and global markets.

SAS Brokerage

Initially formed to support clearance operations and activities for UPS within the kingdom, later SAS Brokerage started to serve other businesses and partners of Eirad Holding Co. and rapidly evolved into a self-sustaining business unit servicing the external customer

* Today SAS is an independent subsidiary of Eirad and performs the following activities
* Customs Clearance of Express and Freight Shipments.
* Customs Clearance for all good on land, sea, and air.
* Specialized team to handle all import shipments for daily volume
* Available at three (3) ports of entry (Jeddah, Riyadh, Dammam – King Fahad Causeway)
* Consultation on documentation and requirements for export and import
* Assisting in local procedures to expedite clearance
