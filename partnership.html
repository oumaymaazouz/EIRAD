Distribution, and Supply Chain Management

Eirad Trading & Contracting Co. Ltd (ETC) is a wholly-owned Saudi company. Exclusive authorized service contractor UPS (United Parcel Service Inc. of USA) in Saudi Arabia since 1997.
ETC/UPS has been offering UPS Express, Domestic Express, and Road Freight Service.
Today, ETC/UPS offers warehousing, logistics, distribution, and consulting solutions to its customers, making it the premiere integrated express shipping, distribution, and supply chain service provider in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
ETC/UPS History synopsis:
* The license was granted in 1996, which was after the exhaustive process!
* Rolled out of Saudi Arabia operation in January 1997.
* WW Exports and Domestic started in July 1997.
* Imports Services started in April 1998.
* In House Customs Clearance by Jpartnershiporldwide. ETC/UPS has created many products and services to meet the
growing demands of the local and global markets.
