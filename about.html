VISION AND PURPOSE

Our vision

To grow our local and global businesses by serving the needs of customers, by offering excellence and value in all that we do. To continue to maintain a financially strong business that provides a long-term competitive return to our shareowners. We inspire our employees and business partners to do their best, by offering opportunities for personal development and success. We lead by example as a responsible, caring, and sustainable company making a difference in the communities we serve.

Our Mission

All businesses and employees of EIRAD will work together and develop products and services that are synonymous with quality, offer excellence and value to our customers, and ensuring profitable returns to our shareowners.

Our Values

Our values are guided by our core belief in upholding our moral and ethical business code of conduct, maintaining our transparent and sustainable business image, and serving the communities we operate in.

CEO MESSAGE

One of the underlying keys to our success has always been that we value the success of partners and customers above all, and the service quality and excellence has always been in the DNA of our structure. We genuinely believe that “giving back” should be one of the most important components of our business endeavor.

Although the Eirad Group has been in existence for more than a quarter of a century, with more than 1,000 employees that work in many of the individual business, subsidiaries, and entities, we still continue to operate as a startup, where we interact with each key members of our team on regular basis and never losing the “personal bond” that we have fostered over the years, and to never negate the fact that Eirad Group is just “one big family” at the end of the day.

As I look ahead with the vision of 2030, I see us leading the way, in not only supporting the realization of “Vision of 2030” but playing an essential part in helping our partners and customers propelling beyond it.

We will continue to stay as a financially viable company that exudes confidence, respect, and trust to our partners, employees, customers and community.

As our company continues to achieve new heights and evolves its operations, it is vital that we must continue to promote a favorable and lasting impression of mutual growth.

Best wishes and regards,

-HRH Prince Sultan Bin Fahad Bin Salman.

LEADERSHIP

Our Corporate System

Our management team has an excellent knowledge of current and pre-requisites to do business in Saudi Arabia particularly when it comes to dealing with multinational businesses. The willingness to adjust to a different environment and launch innovative ideas to make our business more competitive. Eirad Group Management is flexible in their approach to various conditions and can extend that unique managerial style to capitalize on its gains in the marketplace.

We continually invest in our people and ensure that they are professionally trained and possess the required skills and know-how in the fields of their operations.

We are financially sound and a cash positive business group with substantial assets that support our expansion and new ventures and projects. We have excellent relationships with major financial intuition, leading businesses, and local service providers in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Multinational Professional Team

We have a multinational team with extensive experience in managing global businesses, it is through this wide and varied wealth of cultural and professional experience that we can execute our business within the global marketplace. Eirad Group management team manages, coordinates, and supports many multinational and one of the most recognized brands in the world.

Our Local Expertise

Our management team has diverse experience across many industries, private, semiprivate, and government sectors. We have developed strong relationships with many large and medium scale businesses in IT, oil and gas, telecommunications, pharmaceutical, government, banks and financial institutions, construction and FMCG industries.
