EIRAD GROUP

Eirad Group was established in June of 1992, founded under the leadership of the late HRH Prince Fahd bin Salman bin Abdulaziz Al-Saud.

Eirad Group is comprised of wholly-owned companies, subsidiaries, leading business partnerships, international and local investment ventures, and authorized service partners.

Eirad Group is headed by HRH Prince Sultan bin Fahd bin Salman bin Abdulaziz Al-Saud.

Eirad Group strives and is synonymous with integrity, ethical business conduct and for delivering commitments. We take pride in making long-term business partnerships that resonate with trust, transparency and growth.
