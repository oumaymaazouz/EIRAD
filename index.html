EIRAD Group was founded by HRH Prince Fahd bin Salman bin Abdulaziz Al-Saud in June 1992. Over the years, the company has grown with a portfolio of wholly-owned companies, subsidiaries, leading business partnership, international and local investments ventures, and authorized service partners.

Today, HRH Prince Sultan bin Fahd bin Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud, is leading Eirad Group by striving for transparency, integrity, ethical business conduct and delivering commitments.

We take pride in making long-term business partnerships that resonate with trust, transparency and growth.
